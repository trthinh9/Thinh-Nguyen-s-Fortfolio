Developing the Logistics Market in Long Giang Industrial Park

This project focused on expanding logistics services within the Long Giang Industrial Park, a key industrial area in Vietnam. My role involved multiple aspects of business development and operational management.

Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

  • Website Development: Participated in the development of the website sanvantai.viettelpost.vn to enhance online presence and customer engagement.
  • Post Office Management: Directly managed and developed the post office operations within Long Giang Industrial Park.
  • Customer Development: Actively sought and developed corporate customers in the industrial park, expanding the client base.
  • Order Processing: Handled and tracked orders from 54 companies operating in the industrial park.
  • Revenue Growth: Successfully increased revenue from 250 million VND to 600 million VND between 2024 and 2025, demonstrating significant business impact.

Skills Applied:

Logistics Management, Customer Relationship Management, Web Development, Business Development, Operational Efficiency.

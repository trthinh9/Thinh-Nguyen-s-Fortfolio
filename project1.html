Developing the Logistics Market in Long Giang Industrial Park

Project Overview

This project focused on expanding logistics services within Long Giang Industrial Park, a major industrial area in Vietnam. My role covered business development, operations, and customer growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Website Development: Participated in the development of sanvantai.viettelpost.vn to improve customer engagement.
  • Post Office Management: Managed and developed post office operations in the industrial park.
  • Customer Development: Built relationships with corporate customers and expanded the client base.
  • Order Processing: Handled and tracked orders from 54 companies.
  • Revenue Growth: Increased revenue from 250 million to 600 million VND between 2024 and 2025.

Skills Applied

Logistics Management, Customer Relationship Management, Web Development, Business Development, Operational Efficiency.
